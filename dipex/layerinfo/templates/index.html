Welcome

Explore diplomacy using maps. To get started click on a theme named Securing Peace, Respecting Rights, Saving Lives, Protecting Resources, or Promoting Prosperity. These are the five main themes of diplomacy. Each theme has a list of issues and each issue allows you to explore data related to that issue.

In some cases, there will be points that will highlight stories related to the particular dataset.
